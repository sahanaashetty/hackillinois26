Graduation Requirements

  • Minimum 128 hours for graduation
  • Minimum Technical GPA 2.0 (CS and Math courses)
  • Minimum 40 hours upper-division (300–400 level)

General Education

Composition I (4–6h), Advanced Composition (3h), Humanities & Arts (6h), Natural Sciences & Technology (6h), Social & Behavioral Sciences (6h), Cultural Studies (3 courses, 3h each), Quantitative Reasoning (6–10h), Language (3rd level). Some fulfilled by required courses.

      Computer Science Technical Core (35 hrs)

        Technical Electives (min 18 hrs)

        Minimum 6 courses, 18+ hours, from CS 397 and CS 400-level (excluding CS 400, 401, 402, 403, 491). At least one must be a team project course. At least three from a single focus area. CS 498/598 Special Topics may count with approval.

        Team project courses

          Focus areas

          Software Foundations; Algorithms and Models; Intelligence and Big Data; Human and Social Impact; Media; Scientific & HPC; Distributed Systems & Security; Machines. See catalog for full course lists per area.

          Advanced Electives (min 6 hrs)

          Minimum 2 courses, 6+ hours, distinct from technical electives. 400-level letter-grade coursework in any department; CS 397 may apply with limits.

          Free Electives

          Additional coursework to reach 128 total hours, subject to Grainger restrictions.
